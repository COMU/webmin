Postfix
Postfix is Wietse Venema's attempt to provide an alternative to the widely-used Sendmail program. Sendmail is responsible for most of the e-mail delivered on the Internet. With an estimated 100 million users, that's billions of messages daily. A stunning number.

Postfix attempts to be fast, easy to administer, and secure, while at the same time being sendmail compatible enough to not upset existing users.
