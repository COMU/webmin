Profiles
An execution profile is a mechanism used to bundle together the commands and authorizations needed to perform a specific function. Each of the profiles listed on this page has a set of authorizations and a list of sub-profiles whose authorizations it also inherits.

You may want to add additional authorizations to your system if none of those listed here provide the exact level of access control you need. However, existing standard authorizations should be modified carefully, as changing them may effect the privileges of systems users like root and lp.
