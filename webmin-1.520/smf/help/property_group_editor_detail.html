Property Group Editor

The information displayed on this page corresponds to the list of service property groups for both the service and selected instance. Service-level property groups are displayed since their associated properties are inherited by the instance unless they are overriden. Clicking the property group name allows viewing/editing of individual properties within the selected property group. Property groups can also be added/deleted here.
