Backup Schedules
This page lists schedules that can be applied to backup jobs, in order to have them automatically executed by Bacula on a regular basis. Each schedule has a unique name, and a list of backup levels and run times. Each run time must be formatted like mon-fri at 9:00 or sat at 23:00, indicating the days of the week and time to execute. Days can also be specified as days of the month, like 1st sun or on 5. See the Bacula online documentation for the full details of allowed formats.
